# Age（年龄）

### Sentence Structure（句子结构）

- How old are you?
- 译：你多大了？

- When were you born?
- 译：你什么时候出生的?

### Answers（回答）

- I'm 27 years old.
- 译：我 27 岁

- I was born in 1993.
- 译：我出生于 1993 年

- I was born on December 10th,1993
- 译：我出生于 1993 年 12 月 10 日

### Conversation（对话）

- Hey,how are you doing?
- 译：嘿，你好吗？

- Not bad,What about you?
- 译：还不错，你呢

- Good.How old are you?
- 译：我还好。你多大了？

- I'm 27 years old,you?
- 译：我 27 岁，你呢？

- I'm 24 years old.
- 译：我 24 岁
