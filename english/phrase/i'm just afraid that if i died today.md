## I'm just afraid that if I died today, that my life would've amounted to nothing.

#### 单词：

- afraid：害怕
- amounted：总计

#### 连读：

- I'm just afraid that if I died today, that my life would've amounted to nothing.

#### 断词：I'm just afraid that if I died today, that my life would've amounted to nothing.
