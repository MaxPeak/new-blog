# 空闲时间做什么？

### Sentence Structure（句子结构）

- What kind of things do you like doing when you're not working?
  - What kind of things do you like doing when you're not working?
  - 译：你不工作的时候喜欢做什么?
  - Generally I like to go to the gym,but not always.Sometimes I go out with my friends.
  - 译：一般来说，我喜欢去健身房，但也不总是。有时我和我的朋友出去玩。
