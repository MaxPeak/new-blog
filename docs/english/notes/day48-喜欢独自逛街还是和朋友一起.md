# 喜欢独自逛街还是和朋友一起？

### Sentence Structure（句子结构）

- Do you prefer to go shopping alone or with your friends?
  - Do you prefer to go shopping alone or with your friends?
  - 译：你喜欢一个人去购物还是和朋友一起去?
  - It's hard to say,it depends but most of the time I like to shop with my friends.
  - 译：这很难说，要看情况而定，但大多数时候我喜欢和朋友一起购物。
