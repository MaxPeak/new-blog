# Cabin services（客舱服务）

### Sentence Structure（句子结构）

- Would you like ... or ... ?
  - Hello,would you like chicken or beef?
  - 译：你好，您要鸡肉还是牛肉？
  - I'll have the beef please.
  - 译：请给我来份牛肉。
- Can I get ... ?
  - Hi,can I get you anything?
  - 译：你好，我能为你拿点什么吗？
  - Yes,I'm feeling a bit cold.Can I have a blanket?
  - 译：是的，我觉得有点冷。能给我一条毯子吗?
