# Sightseeing（观光）

### Sentence Structure（句子结构）

- Where can we buy tickets?
  - Where can we buy tickets?
  - 译：在哪里买票？
  - The ticket booth is over there.
  - 译：售票处在那边。
- How much are tickets?
  - How much are tickets?
  - 译：票多少钱？
  - 20 dollars for adults and ten for children.
  - 译：成年人 20 孩子半价。
- Where is the gift shop?
  - Where is the gift shop?
  - 译：礼品店在哪？
  - It's next to the exit.
  - 译：在出口的旁边。
