## I wish I had a different life.I wish I was braver and prettier or just happy

#### 译：我希望有不一样的人生，自己能更勇敢、漂亮，至少要快乐

#### 单词：

- wish：希望
- braver：勇敢的
- prettier：更漂亮

#### 连读：

- I wish I had a different life.I wish I was braver and prettier or just happy

#### 断词：I wish I had a different life.I wish I was braver and prettier or just happy
