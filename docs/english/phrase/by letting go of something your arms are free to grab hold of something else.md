## By letting go of something, your arms are free to grab hold of something else

#### 译：放手学习，才能获得更多

#### 单词：

- letting：让（let 的现在分词）
- arms：武器，手臂
- grab：抓住
- free：免费

#### 连读：

- By letting go of something, your arms are free to grab hold of something else

#### 断词：By letting go of something, your arms are free to grab hold of something else
