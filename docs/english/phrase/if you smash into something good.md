## If you smash into something good, you should hold on until it's time to let go

#### 译：如果你碰到美好的事物，应该抓住它，直到必须离开

#### 单词：

- smash：粉碎，碰撞
- until：直到

#### 连读：

- If you smash into something good, you should hold on until it's time to let go

#### 断词：If you smash into something good, you should hold on until it's time to let go
